Agency

Core Values

  1. Meaningful Relationships

    Develop meaningful relationships with clients who are planning meaningful projects. Work with clients — not just for them.

  2. Professionalism

    Produce professional quality deliverables in a professional manner.

  3. Conceptual Design

    Creative begins with a strong concept and is to be executed with that concept as a guide.

  4. Minimalist Design

    Design only what is necessary to successfully communicate the message with the end-user. Everything else is considered a distraction.

  5. Strategic Foresight

    Keep a thumb on the pulse of what is to come. Be in a constant state of learning. Always be planning for the future.

  6. Zero Spec Work

    Do not work for free.
    Do not work for exposure.
    Do not enter design contests.

  7. Family First

    The health and well-being of family will always be more important than any deadline.

  8. Prioritize Mental Health

    Personal health and well-being will always be more important than any deadline.

Process

  1. Discovery

    We say hello, either virtually or in-person. We get to know each other, talk about why we could benefit from working together, and decide whether or not the fit is right.

  2. Estimates & Proposals

    After meeting, we discuss and agree on pricing, timelines and project specifications. A signed contract and deposit are required before work begins.

  3. Project Kickoff

    We go deep into brainstorming project details, your marketing strategy, required technical functionality and project review points.

  4. Project Research

    I go deep into the possibilities.

  5. Design Concepts

    We discuss the findings.

  6. Design Production

    I get to work. We review said work and invoice at the agreed upon stages.

  7. Final Approvals

    Production is finalized and a decision is made on the quality-control of deliverables. Final approval is given and the project is fully invoiced.

  8. Payment & Delivery

    After final invoices are paid, deliverables are set-free into the wild.
